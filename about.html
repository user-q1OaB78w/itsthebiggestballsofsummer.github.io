About Us

We are two individuals with a never-ending love for video games, looking to share our expertise with gamers all around the globe, and aiming to free them from their troubles with poor PC performance. Here, read a bit about us...

Our Story

Being gamers at heart, here at Swills, we've been playing video games for as long as we can remember. Like many of you, our computers were quite weak and could not run modern games, leaving us struggling to keep up with our opponents. We were fed up with losing and knew it was time to take our revenge.

That's where it all began. We delved into the world of optimizations, experimenting with various settings and tweaks to enhance our computers and squeeze more performance out of them. We transformed our computers; the hardware we once believed was rubbish now outperformed PCs with much better components! As our knowledge and expertise grew, we started helping others and saw potential in aiding all gamers around the world, marking the start of our journey with Swills.

Our Mission

Imagine unparalleled latency, as if you were the in-game character, or the smoothness of your game, identical to a cinematic experience. Gamers crave fast, snappy systems, allowing them to prevail during critical situations in gaming. Through Swills, we aim to give everyone the best gaming experience they have ever had: the best performance, lowest latency, and fastest connection out there, helping them beat their rivals.

Our Goals

The target we have set for ourselves is pretty simple: let the whole world know about Swills! Jokes aside, we will be looking to continuously improve and expand Swills, to ensure the best possible user experience. We've got plenty of updates rolling out, and projects we are working on, so make sure to join us for the ride!

Join Our Community

Have any questions for us? Our goal is to ensure your gaming experience is as smooth as possible. Feel free to reach out to us for support at any time on our Discord Server.
